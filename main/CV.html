Requirements

You have learnt HTML language, its tags and about how to build web pages in class lectures. In this assignment your job is to create an online CV of yourself. This CV will include a comprehensive statement of your educational background, work experience, technical skills, professional competencies, life goals, personal interests and your contact details (social media handles etc...)

Your job is to Identify different HTML tags to format the complete CV of yourself. You are free to use any type or number of HTML elements while constructing your CV. But keep in mind that you must include following HTML elements in your solution while developing the CV:

  • Headings
  • Paragraphs
  • HTML lists
  • Table
  • Images
  • Videos
  • Youtube Video (of your own choice related to the context)
  • Audio (May be your favorite motivational quote :)
  • Icons (Search on google on how to insert icons in your HTMI pages and implement the relevant solution yourself)

Every HTML tag usage will weight for equal marks in your total marks.

There is no need to style your pages aggressively using CSS. Just try to create a simple and good looking readable CV just in plain HTML.
